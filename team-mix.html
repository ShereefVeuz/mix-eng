Our Team

Meet our dedicated team ready to help make your next event the best we've ever done.

We’ve always believed that powerful communication is about creating meaning that endures. From the very beginning, our mission has been to design experiences that live on, etched into the memory of every attendee, audience, or partner across the private, public, and not-for-profit sectors.
Nearly two decades in, our mission remains the same. MIX Events has been at the heart of memories and the core of strategies, from creative concepts to bold exhibitions, corporate conferences to entertainment events, sparking a new reality with every step.
We merge creativity, innovation, and precision to deliver moments that spark something that resonates far beyond the now. Working with insights drawn from research and data that grounds our thinking, we create bold paths to better and bigger ideas. 
To us, every client is a world of possibilities, and every project is a chance for us to expand our universe.  As we create the future of events in Saudi Arabia, our legacy continues to grow, much like the events we mastermind: expansive, meaningful, and memorable.

Maha Al-Malik
Honorary President
